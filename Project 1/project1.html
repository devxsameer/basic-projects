IF HTML TAGS WERE PEOPLE

A satirical sterical guide to the quirky personalities of your favorite HTML tags.

  • <div>

    Personality: The chill dude who just vibes with everyone. He's always there but doesn't want attention.

    Favorite Quote: “I contain multitudes.”

  • <span>

    Personality: The stylish one who's all about in-line fashion. Shows up when you need just a *little* flair.

    Favorite Quote: “Why go big when you can go precise?”

  • <a>

    Personality: Always going places. You never know where they'll take you — could be Google, could be the void.

    Favorite Quote: “Click me, I dare you.”

  • <script>

    Personality: The wild card. They bring the drama, the chaos, and sometimes crash the whole party.

    Favorite Quote: “Trust me, bro.”

  • <img>

    Personality: Always photogenic. Doesn't say much, but looks great doing nothing.

    Favorite Quote: “Alt text? Never heard of her.”

  • <form>

    Personality: The people pleaser. Always asking questions, desperate for feedback.

    Favorite Quote: “Submit to me.”

Made with 100% HTML. No CSS, no JS Just vibes.
